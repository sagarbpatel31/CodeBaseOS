# Explore the provenance

More ways to dig in (Command Palette → "CodebaseOS:"):

- **📜 Origin story** — the ordered, cited chain behind a line of code.
- **Compare (with vs without HydraDB)** — graph-grounded answer next to a plain
  LLM with no history. The contrast is the point.
- **Five Whys** — recursive root-cause analysis.
- **Bus factor** — who holds the knowledge, and the risk if they leave.

The **status bar** shows live cost vs the $5 cap, node count, and Merkle
integrity. Open the **dashboard** for the live graph + chaos demo.
